About Rigster

Your trusted partner in custom PC building

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Rigster began as a small PC repair shop with a passion for building custom gaming rigs. Over the years, we've grown into a leading online retailer of PC components and custom-built systems, serving thousands of satisfied customers all over Ireland.

Our mission is to provide PC enthusiasts and gamers with high-quality components, expert advice, and unparalleled customer service. We believe that everyone deserves a PC that's tailored to their needs and budget.
